Lorann

This is an old game from the French Thombson MO5 computer, ported to Java by a team of 3 students from the Exia-Cesi.

The Game

The goal is to take the Energy Ball, and then quit the level using the door. However, the venerable Daemons are blocking your way. Use your spell to beat them!

  • This daemon prefer the attack to the defence. It will try to attack you all the time, not withstanding walls. However, it can't see around a corner; try to beat him with corners!
  • This daemon is very clever, and thus quite dangerous. It will often wait behind a corner to attack you, making him very difficult to dodge. Beat him by your speed!
  • This daemon will scan all the corridors to find you. However, it will often miss splits; attract him, hide in there, and use your spell with a good timing!
  • This daemon will lurk around, without specifically seeking you. However, it goes ways no other daemon will go. Try to find his path; then you can have him easily!

Controls

Upon starting the program, you need to select the level number.

Use your numpad to move, and 5 to fire.

Compiling

Use Eclipse / Maven to compile. You will need Maven, and Maven will install the other dependencies automatically
